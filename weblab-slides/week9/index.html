Introducing AI (Artificial Intelligence)

What is AI

AI refers to the development of computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence..


Which tasks can AI perform?

  • Learning
  • Reasoning
  • Problem-solving
  • Understanding Natural Language
  • Perception
  • Creativity

How does it do it?

Using Natural Language Processing.
NLP is a subfield of AI that focuses on enabling computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language. This involves tasks such as language translation, sentiment analysis, question-answering systems, chatbots, and summarization.

What is ChatGPT?

Chatbot Generative Pre-trained Transformer, is an advanced AI language model developed by OpenAI. It was the first AI tool introduced to the general public, and it remains the most well known chatbot, but now there are many other tools available, including Copilot, Gemini and Adobe Firefly.

How can I use AI?


  • Ask questions related to a project. AI can provide information on a wide range of topics, acting as a useful resource for research projects, essays, or presentations.
  • You can ask for explanations, definitions, or additional information on specific subjects to supplement your research.
  • Use it as a Brainstorming tool.
  • Learn a language or provide translations.
  • Clarify Concepts.
  • It can help you Develop Critical Thinking when you analyze and evaluate the information provided by AI, comparing it with other sources and discussing its accuracy, relevance, and potential biases.
  • Learn more about a personal interest, hobby, or career paths, by asking for information, advice, or resources related to their chosen area of interest.

Drawbacks of using AI


  • AI may sometimes provide incorrect, outdated, or incomplete answers, as it relies on the data it was trained on, which may not always be up-to-date or comprehensive.
    Most chatbots currently combine web search results with their existing data to provide more accurate information, but they may still miss important context or nuances.
  • Since all the content is internet based, it may unintentionally generate biased, inappropriate or offensive content.
  • You may become overly reliant on ChatGPT for answers and guidance, which could hinder the development of critical thinking and independent problem-solving skills.
  • Since it generates text based on existing information, you could inadvertently use content without proper attribution, leading to plagiarism issues.
  • It can occasionally fail to grasp the nuances or complexities of a question or topic, leading to irrelevant or confusing responses.
  • Lack of Human Touch: Empathy, emotional intelligence and cultural sensitivity may not be included in the generated content.
  • Privacy: users' interactions with the AI could be stored or analyzed, potentially exposing personal or sensitive information. Never share personal information with AI.

Prompts for a website

Once you have selected the subject for your website you can prompt AI to help you with:

  • Generating ideas for content
  • Generate catchy headlines
  • Generating the site structure
  • Getting suggestions for colors and fonts
  • Creating a sitemap
  • Creating a wireframe for layout

Sample prompts for a coffee website

  • I want to create a website for coffee lovers.
    Can you suggest some themes I can focus on, based on my main subject?
  • Based on the idea you choose you can expand on it.
    Can you help me define my ideal customer and how to approach them?
  • Can you help me create a content strategy for my website?
  • Can you help me create a sitemap for my website?
  • Can you help me create a wireframe for my website?
  • Can you help me create a color palette for my website?
  • Can you help me choose a typography palette for my website?
  • Can you help me create a catchy headline for my website?
  • Can you help me create a catchy tagline for my website?
  • Can you help me create a list of keywords for my website?
